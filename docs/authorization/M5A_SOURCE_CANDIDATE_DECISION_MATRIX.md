## Recommendation

Recommended first M5B source: `TWSE_OpenAPI` with a bounded TWSE-only target subset such as `2330`, `0050`, and `00929`.

Rationale: it is an official source, lower risk than unofficial/third-party sources, and suitable for a single-source bounded authorization review. This recommendation does not grant authorization, does not issue a token, and does not execute a probe.
