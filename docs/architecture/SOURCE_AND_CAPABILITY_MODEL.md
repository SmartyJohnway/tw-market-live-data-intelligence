# Source and Capability Model

Sources are classified by authority and product role rather than assumed freshness. Capabilities are product surfaces that consume source-derived artifacts.

- Official sources can still be delayed or reference-only.
- Browser endpoints can be useful observation candidates but carry maintenance and semantics risk.
- Commercial or broker/auth sources remain future feasibility candidates unless credentials, contracts, and governance are explicitly approved.
- M5F is canonical Level 1 context; M5K/M5Q are temporary Level 2 context.

See [Source Matrix](../reference/SOURCE_MATRIX.md) and [Capability Matrix](../reference/CAPABILITY_MATRIX.md).
