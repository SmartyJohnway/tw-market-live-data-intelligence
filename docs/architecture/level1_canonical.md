# Level 1 Canonical Context

Level 1 is the M5F canonical market context package at `research/staging/m5f/m5f_canonical_market_context_01/`.

## Contract

- Historical reviewed evidence only.
- Readonly product baseline for local API, MCP, frontend preview, and AI briefings.
- No startup network calls.
- No live refresh, scheduler, polling, or production promotion.
- No mutation by Level 2 live observation.

## Timestamp semantics

Level 1 timestamps describe package generation, source date, evidence lineage, and stale/historical status. They do not prove current market state.

## Consumer rule

Consumers should show Level 1 as canonical context, not a current quote feed.
